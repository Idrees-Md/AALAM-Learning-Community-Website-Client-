My Story

The Inspiring Journey Behind AAAlam Learning Community

"I have always considered AAAlam as my first child. Perhaps that is why I never gave up on it, even during the toughest moments. AAAlam was born on 14th November 2023, with a simple yet powerful intention: to nurture young minds through life skills and practical education using STEAM. What started as a small dream has now successfully crossed two meaningful years."

"For this brainchild of mine, I have invested one of the most valuable resources I own — my time, along with my knowledge, energy, and heart. This journey has taken me to beautiful places, connected me with inspiring people, and most importantly, taught me the true meaning of humility."

"I began this journey alone, but along the way, many wonderful souls believed in AAAlam. They offered their trust, their time, and their energy — and became part of this growing vision. For that, I am deeply grateful."

"Above all, I am thankful to the universe for giving me the opportunity to serve — to work with children, to shape young minds, and to contribute in my own small way to the future. This is only the beginning."

With love and belief,

Jaya Theresa

Founder & CEO, AAAlam Learning Community
