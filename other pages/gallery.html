The Journey of AAAlam

The Beginning – 2023

AAAlam was born on 23rd November 2023 with a powerful intention to nurture young minds through practical education using the STEAM approach. It began with weekend batches and expanded to daily evening tutoring.

First Year Growth – 2024

The first year witnessed workshops, collaborations, and partnerships. AAAlam collaborated with Thinkin Lab and conducted programs at the Regional Science Centre, Coimbatore. The 1st Anniversary was celebrated on 14th November 2024.

Expanding Horizons – Year Two

AAAlam gained recognition through guest lectures, including sessions at CEG, Anna University, and PSGR Krishnammal College. Summer camps and STEAM programs reached students across institutions.

Community Impact

Programs were conducted for Karkai Nandrey NGO and at the Regional Science Centre. Weekend sessions strengthened grassroots learning experiences.

Celebrating the Second Year

AAAlam celebrated its second anniversary at Agaram Iyarkkai Vazhi Velan Pannai, Sulur. A tree was planted to symbolize growth, sustainability, and the future of young minds.
